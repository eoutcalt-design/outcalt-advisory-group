About Outcalt Advisory Group

Experience Built in Real Negotiations

Outcalt Advisory Group was founded on a simple principle:

Most opportunities do not fail because they lack value. They fail because that value is never structured in a way decision-makers can act on.

We specialize in Strategic Acquisition Architecture — the process of transforming ideas, intellectual property, innovations, and businesses into clearly positioned opportunities designed for serious evaluation, negotiation, and execution.

Our role is not to provide generic consulting or surface-level recommendations.

Our role is to help clients create the strategic clarity, buyer logic, positioning assets, and execution frameworks required to move opportunities forward with confidence and authority.

40+ Years of Real-World Deal Experience

Eric Outcalt brings more than four decades of experience in negotiation, sales leadership, financial structuring, operational management, and high-stakes business transactions.

Over the course of his career, he managed automotive dealerships and led complex sales and finance operations in one of the most competitive negotiation-driven industries in the country. Working directly with consumers, lenders, executives, and ownership groups provided a deep understanding of how decisions are made, how opportunities are evaluated, and why many otherwise valuable deals never materialize.

That experience shaped the foundation of Outcalt Advisory Group.

Because successful transactions are rarely driven by enthusiasm alone.

They are driven by:

  • Positioning
  • Clarity
  • Strategic framing
  • The ability to reduce uncertainty for decision-makers

Years spent inside real negotiation environments created a practical understanding of buyer psychology, operational inefficiencies, value perception, and execution strategy that traditional consulting firms often lack.

Why Outcalt Advisory Group Exists

Throughout his career, Eric repeatedly observed the same pattern:

  • Strong opportunities stalled because they were poorly communicated, improperly positioned, or introduced to the market before the foundational structure was in place.
  • Innovators focused on technology but failed to frame commercial relevance.
  • Business owners approached exit conversations before properly preparing the business for evaluation.
  • Complex opportunities were presented without a clear acquisition narrative or buyer ecosystem strategy.

The result was predictable:

  • Reduced leverage
  • Lower perceived value
  • Extended timelines
  • Or complete loss of momentum

Outcalt Advisory Group was created to address that gap.

Beyond Consulting

We do not believe meaningful opportunities are advanced through generic advice alone.

They move forward through structure.

That includes:

  • Strategic positioning
  • Acquisition narrative development
  • Buyer logic alignment
  • Commercialization framing
  • Market preparation
  • Execution planning

Whether the objective is intellectual property commercialization, strategic partnership development, acquisition positioning, or business exit preparation, our focus remains the same:

Build the assets that help serious opportunities get taken seriously.

Our Philosophy

Markets do not reward assumptions. They reward clarity.

Decision-makers respond to opportunities they can quickly understand, evaluate, and act upon.

That is why we focus on transforming complexity into strategic structure — helping clients move beyond abstract potential and toward actionable opportunity.

At Outcalt Advisory Group, we are committed to disciplined thinking, practical execution, and strategic positioning designed for real-world outcomes.

Because ideas alone do not move deals forward. Structure does.
